How Does It Work?

Understand the science and tech behind our mental health survey tool.

The Survey

We use a simplified version of the PHQ-9 questionnaire, a widely used tool in mental health screening. It helps identify signs of depression based on your recent emotional and behavioral state.

Our Classifier

Your answers are processed using a logistic regression model trained on realistic, anonymized data. This model categorizes your response to help you reflect on your mental state — instantly.

Your Privacy

This is a student project, and your input is never stored or shared. We respect your privacy — all processing happens in-memory without saving personal details.

Disclaimer

This tool does not replace professional evaluation. Always consult a qualified mental health provider if you have concerns.
